Tough Mojo Jacket®

  • 100% Polyester
  • Imported
  • Zipper closure
  • Machine Wash
  • Front-zip fleece jacket with standing collar and logo at chest
  • On-seam zippered side-entry pockets
  • HAND WASH COLD / HANG TO DRY / DO NOT IRON / DO NOT DRY CLEAN

Since 1912, we’ve believed in the adventure of a life lived outdoors, the promise of a fair deal, and the guarantee that everything we make is designed to last.

Value starts with quality, which is why we’re vigilant about using materials that are durable, easy to care for, and designed to last. Whether it’s hand-stitched boots or the lightest fly rod imaginable, we want to make sure that you’re getting the best. And because we sell directly to you, it will always be at a reasonable price.

  • This thing is a monster!

    Shaun White
    Review date: 8/10/2016 Rating: 4 stars

    I go to school in Colorado Springs which can be very cold and very very windy sometimes having wind chills up to -40. This coat is great for the cold wet weather. I have worn it in the rain while my jeans were soaked the rest of my body was dry and warm. It's awesome in the wind even when its very cold. The zipper is overlapped with the coat's outer fabric so nothing, not even water. can get through. I have worn it in 25mph wind gusts in 15 degree weather and only wore a t-shirt underneath, and still been warm. Has many pockets and 4 on the inside so you can keep your gloves or whatever warm and dry. Most of all it's very DURABLE, the best I've ever had. The hood is large and the large collar adds even more protection from the elements while the coat itself is very comfortable to wear. Would recommend for anybody who lives in cold wet windy places.
